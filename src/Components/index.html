Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)

Bharatiya Janata Party – Country’s largest political party in terms of representation in the national parliament and state assemblies, and it is the world’s largest party in terms of primary membership. It was founded in the year 1980, April 6th and is led by Mr. Narendra Modi

Congress

Congress’ social policy is based on the Gandhian principle of Sarvodaya. Lifting up of all sections of society for improvement of the lives of economically underprivileged and socially marginalized people. The most praised symbol of a right hand, the palm facing front and fingers pressed together with Indian tricolor background signifies strength, energy and unity.

Aam Aadmi Party

Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) was founded in the year 2012; November 26th By Mr.Arvind Kejriwal & others.It is currently the ruling party of National Capital Territory of Delhi.
